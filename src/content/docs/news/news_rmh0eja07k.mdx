We're excited to announce the launch of **ClassFlow v0.1.0** - now available for installation through AltStore! This marks the first public release of our classroom management app, built from the ground up with Flutter for a smooth, native iOS experience.

## What's New in v0.1.0

This inaugural release brings all the core features you need to stay connected with your classroom:

- **Complete classroom management** with an intuitive, cross-platform UI
- **Background sync support** to keep your classroom data up-to-date
- **Optimized performance** for smooth operation on iOS 12.0 and above
- **Accessibility features** including VoiceOver support
- **Privacy-first design** with opt-in analytics and crash reporting

## Getting Started

ClassFlow is available exclusively through AltStore/SideStore. Simply add this source to your AltStore/SideStore app, find ClassFlow, and tap install. The app will be signed with your Apple ID and ready to use in minutes.

New to AltStore? Visit [altstore.io](https://altstore.io) to get started.

## What's Next

This is just the beginning. We're already working on the next update with enhanced features, performance improvements, and your feedback incorporated. Stay tuned for more announcements!

## Known Limitations

- Background sync is subject to iOS system limitations
- Push notifications will be enabled in a future update
- Free Apple IDs require re-signing every 7 days through AltStore
